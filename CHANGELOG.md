## [5.0.0] - 2016-10-12
### Added
* New Codebase.
* Camera icons on the Traffic Map can now be clustered.

* Added WSDOT North Traffic Twitter account. 
* Added WSDOT Flickr account to Social Media Section.
* Bug report template added in the About section.
